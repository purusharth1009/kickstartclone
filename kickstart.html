Passionate About KVR

With the goal of making job accessibility a fundamental right for the Indian workforce, KVR targets one of the largest gig economy markets in the north. In 2021, we were able to successfully complete more than 2 million tasks with the assistance of more than 75,000 gig workers. Amazon, Flipkart, Jio, Tata Group, Paytm, PhonePe, BharatPe, and Zepto are just a few of the Fortune 500 companies we serve. Noida, India, is where our headquarters are. whereas we have offices in Hapur, Moradabad, Delhi, Bareli, Lucknow, Kanpur, and Indor, making us present all over the nation.

Our business model, innovation strategies, and growth are all technology-centered, and we are a tech-driven company. To gain a competitive advantage and adapt to the market, we use our technology to provide better customer service.

We provide them with more mobility, creativity, and freedom to innovate rather than clinging to conventional procedures as a result of the development of AI technology. Additionally, our cutting-edge technology enables us to attract the greatest number of taskers suited to the task at hand.

Our vision to revolutionize gig jobs digitally for the next billion people, And mission to revolutionize the global gig economy and empower gig workers by making it simpler to get gig jobs.
